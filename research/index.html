Research

The BRAIN RePAIR Lab has expertise in human-induced pluripotent stem cells (hiPSCs) derived from patients with neurodegenerative diseases. Our lab aims to 1) understand the pathologies of various neurodegenerative disorders and 2) develop an AI-based platform for personalized drug discovery.
